The Bishop's School Integration Bee

San Diego's only high school calculus-focused math contest

What is it?

Individual Round

Just like the MIT Integration Bee, you will solve challenging integrals (and similar problems) at a whiteboard head-to-head against your competitor. Fastest correct answer wins! The tournament will take place with a single-elimination bracket consisting of matches of up to fifteen minutes. All integrals can be solved with an AP Calculus BC level of knowledge, but will often require ingenuity and clever tricks under time pressure.

Team Round

Bring a team of up to four students (if you don't have a team, we can make one for you) into a problem set relay race (similar to BMT or HMMT's "guts round") with a live scoreboard. The problems here will be a mashup—some similar to individual round problems—but also including limits, series, calculus-themed trivia, and other calculus problems to spice it up.

Awards

Monetary and book prizes will be given out to the top three contestants from both divisions, along with some merch from our sponsors (including 3Blue1Brown!)

Top team of the Guts round will win prizes and each member of that school attending the integration bee (even non-team members) will get a donut or edible award of the same tier.
